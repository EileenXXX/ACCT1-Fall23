My Dream Place: Snowy Mountain Under Starry Night

Project Brief

As a native of Shanghai, I have rarely witnessed snowfall. However, I absolutely adore skiing and have always dreamed of visiting a picturesque snowy location complete with mountains and snow-covered trees. Furthermore, I am particularly fond of gazing at the starry night sky as it evokes a sense of tranquility within me. Hence, my ultimate dream destination would be a mountainous area blanketed with snow, set against a backdrop of a starry night sky.

Interaction

The dreamland that I created had three cute snowmen, and you can popping up them around the place.

Process

I created this project using Unity. The overall tone and style I went for is a pixelated gaming vibe. To start, I added a button that will take you to my dreamland when clicked. Once there, you will find yourself in a snowy mountain terrain. The highlight of this terrain is the snowmen that you can interact with. Try your best to keep them popping!
